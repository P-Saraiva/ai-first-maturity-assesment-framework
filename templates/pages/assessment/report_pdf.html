Maturity Levels

Each area is evaluated across five maturity levels that reflect how consistently and effectively AI-related practices are implemented:

Level 1
Informal — Ad-hoc level. Practices are reactive and inconsistent; risks often addressed after incidents.
Level 2
Defined — Basic controls exist; practices depend heavily on individuals.
Level 3
Systematic — Processes are documented, standardized, and repeatable; risk, ethics, and security are embedded.
Level 4
Integrated — Practices are measured; decisions informed by data, monitoring, and trends.
Level 5
Optimized — Continuous improvement; lessons and emerging risks are systematically incorporated.
